User Manual

Notes regarding the app features in Teams:

Our App in Microsoft Teams offers support for managing tasks and approvals from our third-party app, further called APP.

This Microsoft Teams app use a conversation bot created with Microsoft Bot Framework to communicate with the users.

Our app allows only licensed accounts, that are preconfigured from our backend and already assigned to an organization.

From our APP, tasks or approvals can be created. Once this app is configured, those tasks and approvals will appear as cards into the configured Team Channel.

This team feature configuration needs to be made once for each feature (task and approval).

When adding this app to your MS Teams, please select Add to a Team option and choose your favorite team to conversate with this app. Permission to the organization level is not supported now.

This app provides access to the following message extensions:

Connect

  • Mention Bot by typing @AHI and select Connect message extension by hitting the up-arrow key.
  • A connect card must be replied to you, by our bot. Please click on the connect button, this is where our third-party app enters.
  • Login using one of the test account email, provided to you.
  • If the connection is successfully, go back to MS Teams app. You should have received a successful message. Further you can configure one of our available features (tasks or approvals) or disconnect from our app.

Disconnect

  • Mention Bot by typing @AHI and select Disconnect message extension by hitting the up-arrow key.
  • Confirm disconnect process and hit Submit button.

Configure a team feature

  • You must be connected to access this command. Connect using connect extension from the previous detailed step.
  • Mention Bot by typing @AHI and select Configure a team feature message extension by hitting the up-arrow key.
  • Select one feature that you want to configure channel for (available options: task or approval)
  • Select one team from the listed ones (these teams are previously created in our APP) to configure
  • Select one channel from the listed ones (these are the available channels of the team where you have installed the app) to receive tasks or approvals on.
  • If successfully, tasks or approvals, will appear on your configured Channel.
